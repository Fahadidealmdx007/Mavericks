FAQ

Everything you need to know

How do I place an order? +
Browse our products, select your size and color, then click "Add to Cart". When you're ready, go to your cart and click "Proceed to Checkout". Fill in your shipping details and place your order.
Can I change or cancel my order? +
You can cancel or change your order within 2 hours of placing it. After that, your order enters production and cannot be changed. Contact us immediately if you need to make changes.
How do I track my order? +
Once your order ships, you'll receive a tracking number via email. You can use this to track your package on the carrier's website.
My order says delivered but I haven't received it. +
Check with your neighbors and building reception first. If it's still missing after 2 days, contact us with your order ID and we'll investigate.
How do Mavericks hoodies fit? +
Our hoodies are oversized by design. If you want the full oversized look, go true to size. If you prefer a more fitted look, size down one size.
Do you have a size guide? +
Yes. Click "Size Guide" on any product page to see chest, length, and shoulder measurements for each size. Our sizes run XS to XL on most pieces.
Are your products unisex? +
Yes, all Mavericks pieces are designed as unisex. Women typically size down 1–2 sizes for a more fitted look, or stay true to size for the oversized aesthetic.
Will my hoodie shrink after washing? +
Our heavyweight fleece may shrink slightly (1–3%) after the first wash. We recommend washing in cold water and air drying to preserve the fit and fabric.
How long does shipping take? +
Domestic (Bangladesh): 3–5 business days. International: 10–20 business days. Express options may be available at checkout depending on your location.
Do you offer free shipping? +
Yes — free shipping on all orders over $120. Orders under $120 have a flat $9.99 shipping fee.
Do you ship internationally? +
Yes. We currently ship to most countries. International orders may be subject to customs fees depending on your country — these are paid by the customer.
My package is stuck in customs. What do I do? +
Customs delays are outside our control. If your package has been held for more than 10 business days, contact us and we'll help you resolve it.
What is your return policy? +
We accept returns within 14 days of delivery. Items must be unworn, unwashed, and in original condition with tags attached. Sale items are final sale.
How do I start a return? +
Contact us with your order ID and reason for return. We'll send you return instructions within 24 hours.
When will I receive my refund? +
Once we receive and inspect your return, refunds are processed within 3–5 business days. It may take an additional 3–7 days to appear on your statement depending on your bank.
Can I exchange for a different size? +
Yes — size exchanges are free. Contact us with your order ID and the size you'd like instead. Subject to stock availability.
What fabric are your hoodies made from? +
Our Double Zipper Hoodies are made from 400GSM heavyweight fleece — 80% cotton, 20% polyester. Built to be soft, warm, and durable enough to last years.
When is the next drop? +
Join our drop list at the bottom of the homepage to get first access to Drop 02 before it goes public. We don't announce dates publicly — drop list only.
Will sold-out items be restocked? +
Rarely — we do limited runs by design. Occasionally popular colorways get a restock, but we don't guarantee it. Join the drop list to be notified if something comes back.
How should I wash my Mavericks pieces? +
Machine wash cold, inside out, with similar colors. Do not bleach. Tumble dry low or air dry. Do not iron directly on prints or graphics.
