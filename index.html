Common questions
Got questions? We've got answers.
Is this official F1 merchandise? +
Mavericks is an independent F1-inspired apparel brand from Bangladesh. We are not affiliated with Formula 1, FOM, or any F1 team. We make original designs inspired by the sport and its culture — not licensed replicas.
Which F1 teams are available? +
Drop 01 covers Red Bull Navy, Ferrari Red, McLaren Papaya, Mercedes Teal, Williams Blue, Alpine Pink, Onyx Black and more. We aim to cover all 10 teams across our drops.
How do the hoodies fit? +
Our hoodies are oversized by design. Go true to size for the full F1-fan look, or size down one for a cleaner silhouette. All pieces are unisex. Check the size guide for exact measurements.
How long does shipping take? +
Domestic (Bangladesh): 3–5 business days. International: 10–20 business days. Orders over $120 ship free. You'll get a tracking number by email once shipped.
When is Drop 02? +
We don't announce drop dates publicly — drop list members get first access and early pricing. Join the drop list to be notified before anyone else.
