Common questions
Got questions? We've got answers.
How do Mavericks hoodies fit? +
Our hoodies are oversized by design. Go true to size for the full oversized look, or size down one for a more fitted silhouette. All pieces are unisex.
How long does shipping take? +
Domestic (Bangladesh): 3–5 business days. International: 10–20 business days. Orders over $120 ship free. You'll get a tracking number by email once shipped.
What fabric are the hoodies made from? +
400GSM heavyweight fleece — 80% cotton, 20% polyester. Built to be soft, warm, and durable enough to last years without losing shape or color.
Can I return or exchange my order? +
Yes — returns accepted within 14 days of delivery if the item is unworn and unwashed. Size exchanges are free. Contact us to start the process.
When is Drop 02? +
We don't announce drop dates publicly — drop list members get first access. Join the list below to be notified before anyone else.
