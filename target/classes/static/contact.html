Quick Answers

How long does shipping take? +
Domestic (Bangladesh): 3–5 business days. International: 10–20 business days depending on location. You'll receive a tracking number once your order ships.
Can I return or exchange my order? +
Yes. We accept returns within 14 days of delivery if the item is unworn and unwashed with original tags. Contact us first and we'll guide you through the process.
Do you do wholesale or bulk orders? +
Yes — for orders of 20+ pieces we offer wholesale pricing. Use the contact form above and select "Wholesale Inquiry" as the subject.
How do I know my size? +
Check our size guide on the product page. Our hoodies are oversized by design — if you're between sizes, we recommend sizing down for a regular fit or staying true to size for the oversized look.
